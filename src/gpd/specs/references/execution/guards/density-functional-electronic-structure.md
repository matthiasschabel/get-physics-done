# Density Functional Electronic Structure Execution Guard

Use for selected `density-functional-electronic-structure` bundles.

- State functional, pseudopotential or basis family, spin treatment, k-point
  mesh, smearing, cutoff, and geometry constraints before comparing energies.
- Check SCF convergence, total-energy variational behavior, force consistency,
  and charge/spin normalization.
- Demonstrate k-point, cutoff, basis, and cell-size convergence for decisive
  energy differences or bands.
- Compare against a trusted material/molecule benchmark, experimental value, or
  higher-level method when the contract requires accuracy.
- Separate numerical convergence, functional error, finite-size/cell error, and
  pseudopotential or basis-set systematics in the SUMMARY.
