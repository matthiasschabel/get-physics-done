# Final Verification Guards

Load this before SUMMARY.md only when selected bundle verification docs do not
cover the result domain. Apply the matching rows and record outcomes in the
self-check section.

| Domain | Final checks |
|---|---|
| QFT / field theory | Ward or Slavnov-Taylor identities, gauge independence of observables, optical theorem/unitarity, crossing or channel consistency, and scheme labels. |
| Condensed matter | Sum rules, Kramers-Kronig relations, fluctuation-dissipation consistency, extensive scaling, and known limiting cases. |
| Statistical mechanics | Positive partition function, thermodynamic stability, Maxwell or Gibbs-Duhem relations, finite-size scaling, and high/low-temperature limits. |
| Numerical / simulation | Observed convergence rate, condition/stability checks, cancellation audit, benchmark comparison, uncertainty bars, and rerun command. |
| General relativity / cosmology | Constraint or Bianchi identities, energy/balance laws, gauge and extraction-radius checks, Newtonian or perturbative limit, and benchmark waveform/solution if available. |
| AMO / molecular / optical | Selection rules, oscillator-strength or sum-rule checks, gauge agreement where relevant, and known transition or molecular benchmark. |
| Fluid / plasma / MHD | Global conservation, CFL or characteristic-speed limit, Reynolds/Lundquist regime, divergence constraint, and benchmark instability or wave mode. |
| Nuclear / particle | Cross-section positivity, partial-wave unitarity, CPT/isospin/flavor checks, normalization convention, and trusted benchmark comparison. |
| Quantum information | Trace preservation, complete positivity, entropy/fidelity bounds, and measurement probability normalization. |
| Astrophysics | Eddington luminosity, virial relation, Jeans or compactness threshold, balance laws, and observational-unit consistency. |
| Soft matter / biophysics | Fluctuation-dissipation relation, osmotic/mechanical stability, scaling exponents, and small-parameter validity. |
| Mathematical physics | Integer/topological invariants, anomaly or index matching, modular consistency, and explicit theorem hypotheses. |

Contract-backed anchors, forbidden proxies, and first-result gates override this
fallback list.
