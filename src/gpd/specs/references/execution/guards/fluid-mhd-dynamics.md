# Fluid And MHD Dynamics Execution Guard

Use for selected `fluid-mhd-dynamics` bundles.

- State equations solved, closure, equation of state, nondimensional numbers,
  boundary conditions, and conserved variables before simulation or derivation.
- Check mass, momentum, energy, entropy production, and magnetic helicity or
  divergence constraints as applicable.
- Enforce the relevant CFL or characteristic-speed condition; for MHD include
  the fast magnetosonic speed, not just bulk flow speed.
- Demonstrate grid/timestep convergence and compare against a benchmark wave,
  shock tube, instability growth rate, or analytic limit.
- Report regime validity: Reynolds, Mach, Lundquist, Knudsen, viscosity,
  resistivity, or ideal-limit assumptions that drive the claim.
