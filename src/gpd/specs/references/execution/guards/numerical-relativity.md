# Numerical Relativity Execution Guard

Use for selected `numerical-relativity` bundles.

- State evolution formulation, gauge, initial data, extraction method, boundary
  treatment, and diagnostic norms before interpreting dynamics.
- Monitor Hamiltonian and momentum constraints at every resolution; growing
  constraints are a physics blocker, not cosmetic error bars.
- Demonstrate convergence for constraint norms and waveform phase/amplitude
  separately.
- Check ADM/Bondi balance, remnant mass/spin consistency, extraction-radius
  sensitivity, and gauge-pathology indicators.
- Compare against analytic perturbative limits, SXS or trusted waveform
  benchmarks, or conserved quantities before claiming strong-field physics.
