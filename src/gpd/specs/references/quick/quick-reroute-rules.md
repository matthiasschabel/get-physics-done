<quick_reroute_rules>
Quick mode must stop instead of producing theorem-style, proof-bearing, or publication-grade evidence.

Reroute theorem-style or contract-backed proof work when the task asks to prove, show existence or uniqueness under named hypotheses, close a theorem/lemma/proposition/corollary, or handle a formal `proof_obligation`. A request for a quick sketch or main idea does not bypass this rule.

Reroute publication-grade verification, referee response, manuscript proof review, or claim-adjudication work to the dedicated verification/publication workflow. Quick output can be useful working evidence, but it is not a substitute for proof-redteam, peer-review, arXiv submission, or manuscript publication gates.

For proof classification details, use `references/verification/core/proof-redteam-workflow-gate.md` only after routing to a proof-aware workflow; do not eager-load that authority in the default quick path.
